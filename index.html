How to get your subscriptions file
  1. Go to takeout.google.com
  2. Click Deselect all, then check YouTube and YouTube Music
  3. Click the All YouTube data included button and select only subscriptions
  4. Click Next step → Create export
  5. Download the .zip when the email arrives — drop it below as-is, or extract subscriptions.csv and drop that instead
